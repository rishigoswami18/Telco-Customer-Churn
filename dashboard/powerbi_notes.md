# Power BI Dashboard Plan

## Page 1: Executive summary

- Total customers
- Overall churn rate
- Monthly revenue
- Revenue at risk
- Top churn drivers

## Page 2: Customer segmentation

- Churn by contract type
- Churn by tenure bucket
- Churn by payment method
- Churn by internet service

## Page 3: Risk heatmap

- Low / Medium / High risk customer counts
- Segment-level churn rate
- Revenue at risk by risk segment

## Page 4: Model output

- Customer-level churn probability
- Retention candidate list
- Filters for contract, tenure, and payment method

## Suggested DAX / visuals

- KPI cards for churn rate, customers, monthly revenue, and revenue at risk
- Clustered bar charts for contract and payment method analysis
- Matrix heatmap for risk segments
- Table visual for retention prioritization
